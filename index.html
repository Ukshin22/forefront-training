How We Helped a 20-Person Sales Team Add £1.2M in New Revenue

A mid-sized B2B technology firm came to us with a consistent problem: their team was generating enough pipeline but closing less than 18% of qualified opportunities. Skilled people — but without a repeatable framework for high-value deals.

We designed a 12-week programme combining advanced closing techniques, enterprise negotiation, and live deal coaching. Sessions were run in small cohorts to maximise engagement and peer learning.
